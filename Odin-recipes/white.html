This white sauce is a quick recipe that will go great as a base for your lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons dairy free butter
  • 1 tablespoon corn flour/gluten free all purpose flour
  • 2 cups dairy free milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup dairy free cheese shreds

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons dairy free butter on medium heat
  2. Once the butter has melted, add gluten free flour*, if using, cook until it starts bubbling
  3. Pour dairy free milk and add a bay leaf then let it come to a boil.
  4. Once the sauce becomes thick, add nutmeg and seasoning to your taste.
  5. Now add the dairy free cheese and stir to mix. Sauce is ready to be used.
